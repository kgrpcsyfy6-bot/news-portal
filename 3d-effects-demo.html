🎨 3D & Interaction Effects — Mockup Gallery

Each demo shows a different visual interaction style. Hover over cards, move your mouse in the dark areas. Pick what you like.

1. 3D Card Tilt — Perspective + Depth

Cards tilt toward your cursor like a physical card on a table. Uses CSS perspective + transform: rotateX/Y. No Three.js — pure CSS/JS, ~2KB. Works on all modern browsers. Subtle gloss overlay follows light angle.

2. Mouse Trail — Particle Cursor Effect

A trail of glowing particles follows the cursor on dark backgrounds. Canvas-based. Works as a subtle "data flow" or "connection" metaphor for tech/business sections. Could also work on the hero area or as a page-wide subtle effect.

Move your mouse here → particles follow

3. Card Glow Follow — Radial Gradient on Mouse Position

A subtle red glow follows your cursor position inside each card. No 3D math — just a radial gradient positioned at the mouse. Very lightweight, very subtle. Good for "this is interactive" without being distracting.

4. Three.js 3D Globe — Interactive Earth

A rotating globe built with Three.js (~140KB gzipped CDN). Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom. Glowing dots mark news hotspots. This could be a hero background or a sidebar widget. Matches the news globe concept.

Drag to rotate · Scroll to zoom

5. Combined: Tilt + Glow Follow (Recommended)

Cards have subtle 3D tilt AND a soft red accent glow that follows your cursor. Best of both worlds — the physicality of 3D with the elegance of the glow. This is the recommendation for production.
